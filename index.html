About the game

Testing your knowledge should always be a fun game. Let's see what we've learned so far from SEI course and if we're able to speak the engineering lingo in the real world.

How to play

  • Similar to Jeopardy, you select the category and a prize amount box to reveal your question in an answer format.
  • You then select the best multiple choice that matches the answer.
  • If you selected the correct answer, your score will increased by the question amount.
  • If you selected the incorrect answer, your score will be decreased by the question amount.
